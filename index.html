What We Do

Five areas where we make a real difference.

We work across the full lifecycle of a manufacturing or trading business — from setting one up to turning it around, from raising capital to navigating legal distress.

When people want to start a new manufacturing business, they often don't know where to begin. We guide founders through the full journey — from advising whether a business is viable, to capital structuring, banking relationships, and eventually setting up the manufacturing unit itself.

We don't just give a plan. We walk alongside you — from the first idea to the first day of production.

Companies come to us with all kinds of problems — low production, quality failures, productivity slumps, labour friction. We send our team on the ground, understand the process, speak to the workforce, and get things moving. We have doubled productivity in under a week.

We also worked with a hospital burning cash, facing eviction, with no accounting or documentation. Over 12 months we restructured their banking, negotiated expenses, secured a loan, and restructured the board. Revenue doubled.

We help companies understand their true capital requirements and navigate bank relationships efficiently. We don't always recommend raising capital — sometimes the honest advice is that the business needs to change first. We'll tell you that plainly.
